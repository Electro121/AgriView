How AgriView Works

A comprehensive platform built on cutting-edge technology and real-world agricultural science

1

Intuitive User Interface

Make informed decisions through our streamlined dashboard:

  • Select climate-appropriate crops (Rice or Maize)
  • Adjust irrigation levels with precision controls
  • Optimize fertilizer application rates
  • Run comprehensive season simulations instantly
2

Advanced Simulation Engine

Our algorithm analyzes multiple factors to predict outcomes:

  • 3-month historical NASA climate data (rainfall & temperature)
  • User-defined irrigation and fertilization strategies
  • Crop-specific parameters including water needs and drought resistance
  • Sustainability optimization balancing yield and resource conservation
3

NASA Data Integration

Powered by three critical satellite data sources:

  • POWER API: Historical rainfall and temperature data for Dhaka coordinates
  • SMAP: Real-time soil moisture monitoring in the root zone
  • MODIS: NDVI vegetation index for crop health assessment
4

Intelligent Feedback System

Receive actionable recommendations when:

  • Sustainability thresholds are at risk
  • Yields can be optimized through resource adjustment
  • Over-irrigation or excessive fertilization is detected
  • Climate conditions suggest alternative strategies
